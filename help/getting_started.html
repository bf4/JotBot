Welcome to JotBot, a different take on time tracking.

JotBot is a "pull" time tracking application. Unlike most systems that expect you to remember to make regular time entries throughout the day, JotBot comes to you and unobtrusively asks you to record what you're working on.

If this is your first time running JotBot, we recommend viewing the tour of basic features or watching the getting started video on our website at http://getjotbot.com.

If you're having problems with JotBot, please contact us at support@neurogami.com.
